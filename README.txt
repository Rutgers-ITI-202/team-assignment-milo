Welcome to Milo!

You are Fuze, Captain of the King's Royal Banana Guard, sent into enemy territory to recover your King who is being held 
in the Temple of Forbidden Monkeys. 
Find your way through a maze of rooms to collect items to free King Milo! Find and collect all the items to win!
Do you have what it takes to free your king?

Commands:

Movements:
n = move north
s = move south
e = move east
w = move west

look = get description of the room
get [item] = type "get" followed by the name of the item to pick it up
inventory = see what items are in your inventory
quit = end the game
